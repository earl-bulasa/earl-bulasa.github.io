Dentures are removable replacements for missing teeth. Compared to other dental treatments like implants, patients choose affordable dentures. It’s less expensive and has more efficient masticatory functions.

There are two types of dentures that are available for each patient’s specific dental needs.

Complete dentures

A complete denture is the option if the entire set of teeth is missing. The dental clinic in London Ontario offers either conventional or immediate dentures. Conventional types will be given 8 to 12 weeks after healing of the gums. Meanwhile, immediate dentures will be given right after tooth extraction.

Partial dentures

Partial dentures are more affordable dentures than the complete type as it replaces only a few missing teeth. It consists of the replacement teeth attached by metal wires connecting to the gum-colored plastic base.

How to find affordable and effective dentures

It’s easy to find cheaper dentures. What’s challenging is to find affordable ones that don’t compromise the denture’s quality. The dentists at a dental clinic in London Ontario advise patients to look for affordable and durable dentures by doing the following:

  • Check out the new clinics in the area. Aside that there’s less expense in transportation, new clinics offer affordable dentures as part of their promotional strategies. The price tag may change however, long-time clients avail discounts and flexible payment plans.
  • Try the dental schools. Dental students look for patients whom they can test their dental skills at an affordable rate. Don’t worry, dentist instructors supervise them during the procedure. Students also aim to provide top performance to pass the program.
  • Experience dental tourism. Some patients consider flying out of the country for business and pleasure. Try vacationing and locating affordable dentures at Mexico at the same time.

The dental clinic in London Ontario has negotiable payment options to ensure their parents can have immediate dental treatment.
