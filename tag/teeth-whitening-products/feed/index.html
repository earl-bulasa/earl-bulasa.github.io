Malocclusion commonly happens to patients who have developed mannerisms. It can also happen to anyone due to his medical history. Buck teeth causes need improvement to prevent dental issues such as the following:

  • Speech impediments. Due to the gaps and protrusion of the teeth and lips, a patient can suffer lisping. This is the condition where someone can’t properly pronounce words with letters such as S, R, V or T.    
  • Chewing defect. At London Ontario Dental Clinic, dentists help patients correct their buck teeth because they can’t eat correctly.   
  • Sleep apnea. The buck teeth itself is not the main cause for this sleeping condition. However, most patients with buck teeth, causes crowded teeth and smaller jaw size that limits airway passage.  

Causes of buck teeth

  1. A patient’s genetic material can affect the way his teeth structure may turn out. If most of his ascendants have buck teeth, he’s likely going to inherit it according to the London Ontario Dental Clinic.
  2. Sometimes, natural large teeth can be mistaken as buck teeth. It appears protruding and has wide spaces that they look like overbites.
  3. Thumb-sucking and pacifier use causes problems if the child continues the habit after he has permanent teeth.
  4. Tongue thrusting. This is one of the buck teeth causes that forces the teeth to arch forward and misalign.
  5. Jaw clenching and teeth grinding. When these conditions became habitual, it gradually fractures the teeth or causes it to protrude.

Treatment

  • Braces or aligners – are effective ways to treat buck teeth and other misalignment issues. These dental devices have the ability to correct the teeth with their mechanism.
  • Oral maxillofacial surgery – is another treatment option available at the London Ontario Dental Clinic. This treats serious buck teeth cases that involve skeletal structure.

Cosmetic dentistry became the most requested dental service due to the influence of society today. Patients request for cosmetic dentistry procedures to improve their teeth appearance. The options are vast from covering tooth cracks to spaces and even replacing a lost tooth.

Most of these dental procedures are usually for aesthetics and not for essentials. However, the results also give restorative advantages.

There are various dental cosmetic therapies at London Ontario Dental Clinic that helps patients attain a beautiful smile.

Types of cosmetic dentistry procedure

Teeth whitening

Teeth bleaching or whitening is the most common cosmetic procedure to brighten a patient’s teeth. It’s done at the dentist’s office or at home using teeth whitening products. These cosmetic dentistry procedures can clear away teeth stains from coffee or tobacco use.

Bonding

Teeth bonding uses tooth-colored composite materials to cover a decayed or discolored tooth. It’s one of the most affordable way to treat tooth cracks and chips and even gaps.  

Veneers

The London Ontario Dental Clinic have custom-made dental shells or veneers. They’re made of ceramic materials to look realistic in covering worn down and stained teeth.

Inlays and onlays

When a tooth is badly damaged and has broken most of its structure, it can no longer support a simple filling. This is the time to use inlays and onlays, also called as indirect fillings, to cover a tooth crack.

Implants

Cosmetic dentistry procedures such as implants restore a patient’s missing tooth and his confidence. It is made of titanium material that serves as a tooth root. On top of it, the dentist places a denture or a crown. Dental implants is proven durable and long-lasting for most patients.

There are more related dental procedures that can bring back a patient’s proud smile. Patients may consult their dentists at London Ontario Dental Clinic to check out their options.

Why choose dental implants?

The best dentist in London Ontario shares why dental implants are the perfect solution for missing tooth replacement.

  • Prevents bone loss and premature aging. The moment a tooth is lost, the jaw bones start to adjust and misalign making a patient appear older. With affordable dental implants, the facial structure keeps young and firm.  
  • Long-lasting investment. The results for having dental implants are worth its price. It may be costly than other dental treatments, however, the security of a weariless smile it provides is lifelong.

Teeth whitening products are widely used these days as companies produce more of its kind. Patients buy these since they’re cheaper and easier to maintain as when you see a dentist for a dental cosmetic procedure. It makes non-users wonder if teeth whiteners are really effective.

According to the dentists at Stoney Creek Family Dental, patients suffer teeth discoloration for multiple reasons:

  • Tea, coffee, and wine are examples of drinks that cause stains to your teeth. They contain color pigments that are strong enough to easily attach and stay on the tooth’s enamel.
  • Smoking tobacco is one habit that’s bad for both general and dental health. It’s composed of a dark tar and nicotine that creates a yellowish stain when mixed with oxygen. Smokers need to use tough teeth whitening products consistently to remove teeth stains.
  • The older the patient is, the weaker his tooth enamel becomes. The patient’s yellowish dentin becomes more visible when the hard surface of the tooth wears off.
  • The tooth’s dentin also reacts when there’s facial trauma. Injury to the mouth often results in a black tooth.
  • The Stoney Creek Family Dental also finds certain medications that affect a tooth’s color. Antihistamines and antipsychotic medicines may have teeth tingeing components. Children’s developing teeth may also acquire intrinsic stain due to fluoride overuse.

How do teeth whitening products work?

These teeth whitening products contain bleaching agents called carbamide peroxide or hydrogen peroxide. These chemicals penetrate and break down tooth blemishes into tinier particles. With this process, the discoloration gradually fades, leaving brighter and whiter teeth.

Their dentists perform chairside bleaching, a common in-office teeth whitening treatment, done in a single visit. They can also recommend effective teeth whitening products for you!
