The Toronto Discovery District is one of the most renowned centers of biomedical research, technological innovation and education in North America. More good news for local residents is that the district contains numerous popular restaurants, shopping venues and abundant services of nearly every kind. The following list is a sample of what is in store for you if you visit.

Shopping

Whether you are looking for a stylish new suit or want to purchase the latest technological gizmo, Toronto Discovery District is the place to get it. Take a walk in the area to discover the following shopping venues and more!

  • Club Monaco – men’s and women’s clothing
  • Sephora – cosmetics and beauty supply
  • Cole Haan – shoes
  • David’s Footwear – shoes
  • William Ashely China – kitchen and bath
  • 7 Chanel Boutique – accessories, women’s clothing and cosmetics
  • Bay Bloor Radio – electronics
  • Kensington Market – farmer’s market with vintage items and local flair
  • Toronto Eaton Centre – shopping mall
  • Harry Rosen Gentlemen’s Apparel – men’s clothing and shoes
  • Montblanc – leather goods, accessories and watches
  • Tiffany and Company – fine jewelry
  • Lavish and Squalor – men’s and women’s clothing
  • Rolo – gift shop

 

Dining

With thousands of students, professionals and employees working busily every day in the district, it is no wonder there are so many excellent restaurants to accommodate them. From fine dining restaurants to convenient sandwich shops, you can always find a dining experience you can enjoy.

  • Sansotei Ramen – Ramen cuisine – casual dining
  • Japango – Sushi, Japanese, Asian – casual dining
  • Kyoto House – Sushi – casual dining
  • Eat Fresh Be Healthy – healthy food, fusion – casual dining
  • Lai Wa Heen – Cantonese, dim sum – fine dining
  • Bare burger – burgers – fast food
  • Yueh Tung – Hakka, Chinese – casual dining
  • Spadina Garden – Sichuan – casual dining
  • Tosto Quickfire Pizza Pasta – Italian – casual dining
  • Courtyard Arms – Canadian cuisine – fine dining
  • New Yorker Deli – deli and breakfast
  • Mercatto – Italian – casual dining
  • Epicure City Mt. Fuji – Asian, Japanese, sushi – casual dining

 

Art

On the edge of the district, you can find the Art Gallery of Ontario. With over 2000 prized works of art, the gallery draws visitors from all over world.

 

Services

Surviving in today’s high-tech, hurry-scurry world is much easier when you can rely on nearby service businesses to help you. Toronto’s Discovery District is home to many companies that serve the general public. Service businesses in the district include florists, dry cleaners, caterers and tutoring services, among others.

  • Flowers on Bay
  • Wilbe Bloomin
  • Corporate Crazy Caterer
  • Pumpernickel’s Mars
  • Discovery Cleaners
  • Belair Dry Cleaners
  • Brieffest Tutoring Center

 

Professionals

Professional services are conveniently located throughout the Toronto Discovery District. There, you can find legal representation for your personal and professional interests. Doctors abound in this area where biomedical research and services take center stage. You can find dentists at clinics like Downtown Dentistry oral surgeons and other dental care specialties. In addition to these physical health professionals, there are also psychiatrists, psychologists, therapists and business coaches to meet your emotional, social and business needs.

Life in Toronto’s Discovery District is both comfortable and exciting. It brings new opportunities to experience good food, gracious service and wonderful shopping adventures. When you also consider the wide range of personal and professional services available to you, you are sure to be happy you came!

    Eaton Centre is a premier shopping destination in Toronto, but it is much more than that. It is a location where you can find restaurants, theaters and a wide variety of services. The following list is a sampling of dentists you can find in the convenient downtown area near Yonge and Dundas, where Eaton Centre is located.

    Downtown Dentistry

    Location: 438 University Avenue, Suite 1800
    Dentist: Dr. Amelia Chan
    Travel time by car from Yonge and Dundas: 1 minute

    Dr. Amelia Chan, head of Downtown Dentistry, along with associates Dr. Gina Ha, Dr. Somayeh Nourian, and Dr. Brian McLean, offer complete dental services. The focus at this clinic is dental health and wellness. In addition to general dentistry services, Dr. Chan focuses on cosmetic dentistry, periodontal surgery, orthodontics and implants.

     

    Dentistry In Toronto

    Location: Rosedale Medical Center at 600 Sherbourne St. Suite 815
    Dentist: Dr. Janice Goodman
    Travel time: 2 minutes

    With over 30 years practicing in the Toronto area, Dr. Goodman is a well-regarded member of the dental community. Dr. Goodman is known for excellence, particularly in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. Toronto Life Magazine named this clinic as the best dental office in Toronto.

     

    Bay Bloor Dental

    Location: 151 Bloor Street West, Suite 860
    Dentist: Dr. Sherman Ki
    Travel time: 3 minutes

    Dr. Ki is an experienced dentist who has served the Toronto area for over 12 years. With degrees from the University of Toronto and the University of Manitoba culminating in a Doctor of Dental Medicine (D.D.M.) degree, Dr. Ki is well-equipped to meet dental needs from routine care to dental surgery. The location near Eaton Centre is convenient, and the office staff are both helpful and friendly.

     

    St. Raphael’s Dental Care

    Location: 215 Victoria Street
    Dentist: Dr. Maroulla Barsoum
    Travel time: 1 minute

    Dr. Barsoum is committed to helping you keep your teeth while offering smile enhancement services to help you enjoy them more fully.

     

    Dr. Yolanda C. Cruz

    Location: The Sheraton Centre Hotel, Richmond Tower, 100 Richmond Street West, Suite 443
    Dentists: Dr. Yolanda Cruz, DDS, Dr. Natalie Wong, DDS, Dr. Anil Rick Soordhar, DDS
    Travel time: 1 minute

    Among the three dentists in this clinic, you can find excellent providers of dental services including general dentistry, Lumineers, Invisalign, periodontal treatments, prosthodontics, implants, orthodontics and more.

     

    Jane Clair Dental Clinic

    Location: 2698 Jane Street
    Dentist: Dr. Fred Erbiceanu
    Travel time: 17 minutes

    Jane Clair dental is a bit farther from Eaton Centre than some clinics, but the trip is well worth a few extra minutes when you find a dentist who is right for you. Dr. Erbiceanu provides dental services that range from routine checkups to root canal therapy to TMJ/TMD treatments.

     

    West Metro Dental

    Location: 191 The West Mall (at Dundas)
    Dentist: Dr. Sandeep Walla, Dr. Shah
    Travel time: 18 minutes

    West Metro Dental is a friendly office offering caring treatment for dental patients in Toronto. Dr. Walla provides basic restorative services, cosmetic dentistry and implants. Dr. Shah also provides a variety of dental services. The clinic prefers conscious sedation to help relieve their patient’s dental anxiety and dental phobiaand make their office a fear-free zone. It’s all at the West Metro Dental Office, not far from Eaton Centre.
