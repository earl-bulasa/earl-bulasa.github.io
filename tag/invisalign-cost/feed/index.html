Invisalign revolutionized the teeth alignment when it came out. Virtually being invisible, most wonder how much more Invisalign cost than braces.

WHAT IS INVISALIGN?

Invisalign – the virtually invisible braces that correct misaligned teeth without giving sores and dental aches to the patient. Dentists make sure that they make it based from the patient’s teeth. They carefully make it to make sure it corrects and gives comfort to the patient. A top dentist in Toronto Ontario adds that results begin showing after two weeks of use.

HOW IS INVISALIGN DIFFERENT FROM BRACES?

While Invisalign appears nearly invisible, braces appears 100% visible even while away from the patient using it. Invisalign cost more because involves a clear plastic device molded according to the patient’s teeth. Braces use metal brackets that dentist glue on to the patient’s teeth making sure it does not move during the entire period.

Invisalign needs to be worn at least 22-24 hours a day for an average of 1.5 years. This changes depending on what the patient needs and what a top dentist in Toronto Ontario recommends. Patients wear braces all the time for at least two (2) years without removing it.

Some patients like using braces. When patients customize the appearance of their braces, it becomes their own fashion statement or aesthetic. Patients choose the color of the metal brackets, rubber bands, and the wires. They ask their dentists to put this in their mouths and become a part of their whole aesthetic.

DOES INVISALIGN COST MORE THAN BRACES?

Fortunately, yes. Invisalign cost more than braces. The average cost for Invisalign ranges from a minimum of CAD 3,500 and this goes up depending on a number of factors. Some of the factors include expertise and knowledge coming from a top dentist in Toronto Ontario, the patient’s needs, and location of the dentist’s clinic.

Always remember to consult with your dentist before choosing the treatment. They will give you the best pieces of advice when it comes to Invisalign or braces.
