Eaton Centre is a premier shopping destination in Toronto, but it is much more than that. It is a location where you can find restaurants, theaters and a wide variety of services. The following list is a sampling of dentists you can find in the convenient downtown area near Yonge and Dundas, where Eaton Centre is located.

Downtown Dentistry

Location: 438 University Avenue, Suite 1800
Dentist: Dr. Amelia Chan
Travel time by car from Yonge and Dundas: 1 minute

Dr. Amelia Chan, head of Downtown Dentistry, along with associates Dr. Gina Ha, Dr. Somayeh Nourian, and Dr. Brian McLean, offer complete dental services. The focus at this clinic is dental health and wellness. In addition to general dentistry services, Dr. Chan focuses on cosmetic dentistry, periodontal surgery, orthodontics and implants.

 

Dentistry In Toronto

Location: Rosedale Medical Center at 600 Sherbourne St. Suite 815
Dentist: Dr. Janice Goodman
Travel time: 2 minutes

With over 30 years practicing in the Toronto area, Dr. Goodman is a well-regarded member of the dental community. Dr. Goodman is known for excellence, particularly in cosmetic and restorative dentistry. Toronto Life Magazine named this clinic as the best dental office in Toronto.

 

Bay Bloor Dental

Location: 151 Bloor Street West, Suite 860
Dentist: Dr. Sherman Ki
Travel time: 3 minutes

Dr. Ki is an experienced dentist who has served the Toronto area for over 12 years. With degrees from the University of Toronto and the University of Manitoba culminating in a Doctor of Dental Medicine (D.D.M.) degree, Dr. Ki is well-equipped to meet dental needs from routine care to dental surgery. The location near Eaton Centre is convenient, and the office staff are both helpful and friendly.

 

St. Raphael’s Dental Care

Location: 215 Victoria Street
Dentist: Dr. Maroulla Barsoum
Travel time: 1 minute

Dr. Barsoum is committed to helping you keep your teeth while offering smile enhancement services to help you enjoy them more fully.

 

Dr. Yolanda C. Cruz

Location: The Sheraton Centre Hotel, Richmond Tower, 100 Richmond Street West, Suite 443
Dentists: Dr. Yolanda Cruz, DDS, Dr. Natalie Wong, DDS, Dr. Anil Rick Soordhar, DDS
Travel time: 1 minute

Among the three dentists in this clinic, you can find excellent providers of dental services including general dentistry, Lumineers, Invisalign, periodontal treatments, prosthodontics, implants, orthodontics and more.

 

Jane Clair Dental Clinic

Location: 2698 Jane Street
Dentist: Dr. Fred Erbiceanu
Travel time: 17 minutes

Jane Clair dental is a bit farther from Eaton Centre than some clinics, but the trip is well worth a few extra minutes when you find a dentist who is right for you. Dr. Erbiceanu provides dental services that range from routine checkups to root canal therapy to TMJ/TMD treatments.

 

West Metro Dental

Location: 191 The West Mall (at Dundas)
Dentist: Dr. Sandeep Walla, Dr. Shah
Travel time: 18 minutes

West Metro Dental is a friendly office offering caring treatment for dental patients in Toronto. Dr. Walla provides basic restorative services, cosmetic dentistry and implants. Dr. Shah also provides a variety of dental services. The clinic prefers conscious sedation to help relieve their patient’s dental anxiety and dental phobiaand make their office a fear-free zone. It’s all at the West Metro Dental Office, not far from Eaton Centre.
