A dental crown is also referred to as dental caps which are used for restorative treatments. This replaces damaged, decayed and discoloured parts of your teeth. Its main purpose is to save your teeth and preserve its functions. This also strengthens your bite because crowns are made from durable elements. Materials used include composite resins, ceramic and porcelains that can be infused with stronger materials such as metals.

Your Toronto dentist performs the dental crown procedure which involves trimming down your natural tooth. Its purpose is to hold your dental cap in place with the help of a special bonding agent. Here are the other amazing facts you need to know about dental caps or crowns.

FACTS ABOUT DENTAL CROWN

If you’re considering for a dental cap, it is best to consult your prosthodontist about this procedure. You have to ensure that it is really what’s suited for your teeth’s need. Here are some important information you need to know prior to getting this treatment.

1. NOT FOR ALL SITUATIONS.

A dental crown is reserved to fix badly decayed or severely discoloured teeth. Majority of the less serious cases can be fixed by your dentist using treatments such as fillings or veneers.

2. LONG TERM SOLUTION.

Dental caps are long term solution for your dental problems. This is because the crowns are made from strong materials such as porcelain, ceramic and composite resins. They usually last you for a period ranging from 20 -30 years, depending on how you take care of them. Keep in mind that teeth caps that are made from composite resins can stain.

3. DURABILITY AND FUNCTIONALITY.

The dental crown procedure would need durable materials to ensure that crowns are strong and functional. Porcelain is one of the preferred materials for dental caps because it effectively enhances the strength of your natural teeth. Most dentists recommend this to treat severe damage and teeth injury. Porcelain caps can also be infused with gold to provide more strength and durability.

4. NATURAL APPEARANCE.

In today’s advancement in dentistry, matching the colour of your dental crown to your natural teeth is achieved flawlessly. It is crafted by your orthodontist to be natural looking and aesthetically pleasing. The caps do not only match the colour but also the teeth shape. It is really hard to tell the difference of a dental cap from your natural teeth. Thanks to this advancement, restoring your teeth does not only enhance your smile but also your confidence.

5. PROCESS.

Traditionally, the dental crown procedure takes several visits. The first visit will entail decay removal, teeth cleaning and shaping. Your dentist will also take impressions of your teeth for the shaping of the crowns. A temporary crown will be placed to ensure that you do not leave the clinic with a broken smile.

On the second visit, your dentist will remove the temporary crown and fit the permanent one. Some adjustments may be necessary prior to finally cementing the crown to your natural teeth.

Nowadays, many clinics have advanced digital machines that can take an impression via specialized cameras. This helps shorten the traditional steps in this treatment such as the manual taking of impressions from mouth trays. With the help of this technology, having dental caps can be done in one single visit.

Check out if your dental clinic uses an advanced technology in making same day crowns. The tooth crown cost should be the least of your concern since this is the most affordable restorative option. See you dentist today to learn more about this treatment.

    Laser dentistry uses a light energy beam technology that’s so narrow and focused it could treat various dental conditions. Even those that include an extensive and complicated procedure. It became commercially accepted for clinical practice that especially involves soft dental tissues in 1989. This is a great approach for patients who want to avoid pain or discomfort when going through a dental treatment.

    Furthermore, the laser light produces a reaction when it touches your gum tissues. This breakthrough allows the reshaping and gum contouring for both restorative and cosmetic purposes.

    Your Toronto dentist uses laser for several treatments and procedures. This includes laser dental cleaning, laser teeth whitening and periodontal or gum disease treatment.

    BENEFITS OF LASER DENTISTRY

    One of the reasons why the use of laser is integrated in most dental practices is because of its multiple benefits. These include the following:

    1. NON-INVASIVE

    The key defining feature of laser dentistry is that it is non-invasive, less strident and more relaxing to patients. This is due to the lasers being virtually silent as compared to using traditional dental tools. This is an effective method to break the stigma about dental procedures and encourage everyone to see their dentists regularly.

    2. ACCURACY.

    Laser dental cleaning is way more accurate than traditional teeth cleaning techniques. Since the beams could precisely pinpoint any targeted area, it offers high success rate for any dental treatment.

    Unlike the bulbous instruments, laser light would only touch the tissue or the area where it is needed. Especially during root canal treatment, it’s easy to remove an infection. Moreso, this technology achieves a minimal to zero damage at all.

    3. LESSER RISKS.

    Patients used to suffer post-procedural risks because of infection brought about by traditional tool wounds. When you go home, you need to observe difficult oral regimen just to ensure that your newly-treated tooth is sanitized.

    Since laser dentistry also have disinfecting features, it minimizes bleeding and lessens exposure to infection and disease-causing bacteria.

    4. REDUCED NEED FOR ANESTHETICS.

    As mentioned, laser technology is non-invasive and the patient mostly feels pressure and not pain during the process. Thus, the need for sedation or anesthesia is reduced or removed altogether. The beam light beats most of dentistry’s famous dental equipment including scalpels and scalers.

    5. FASTER HEALING.

    With laser dental cleaning, the gum tissues heal faster due to the light sterilizing the gum area while being treated. Since the gum is sterile, the tissue regenerates quicker. This makes the recuperating period minimal and you will soon be able to get back to your regular routine.

    6. EFFECTIVENESS.

    Avoiding the progression of gum disease from gingivitis is the number one priority of your orthodontist. This is because he wants to save as much gum tissue as possible.

    Commonly, scaling and root planing is the go-to treatment for these diseases. This treatment uses a scalpel and electrosurgery device that uses high-frequency waveforms or currents. The use of laser allows your dentist to fix your gum disease, eliminate the toxin, and completely remove the bacteria. The laser light sterilizes your gum area, which is why this is the most recommended solution for this dental procedure.

    7. SAFE.

    Avoiding vibrations and direct contact with your teeth and its roots make laser treatment safer than the traditional dental cleaning. The accuracy of the laser light technology keeps all other dental tissues safe. No over reduction is made, that’s why the surround teeth and gums are safe.

    Advancement in dentistry has led to using the laser as a great alternative to conventional procedures. Now, there’s no reason for you to hesitate in going through the dental treatment. Visit your local dental clinic to learn more about this technology.

      Dental veneers are restoration appliances that are added on the front surface of your teeth. This aims to correct teeth discolouration, minor misalignments, chipping and cracks. It is a treatment that is commonly used to aesthetically enhance the look of your teeth.

      Composite resin and porcelain dental veneers are two types of shells that your dentist uses for the restoration procedure. Today, more patients are eyeing to have dental veneers in Toronto, Ontario to improve their smiles. If you’re one of them, here are a few things you need to know before you go through this treatment. Read on to know what are the things you need to include on your checklist.

      THINGS TO KNOW BEFORE HAVING DENTAL VENEERS

      There are a few things you need to consider before you decide to have dental veneers. Aside from the fact that this treatment involves reshaping of your teeth, here are other information you need to know:

      1. YOU NEED AN EXPERT TO ENSURE FANTASTIC RESULTS.

      This cosmetic procedure needs precision and expertise for you to gain excellent results. You need to make sure that your dentist is an expert in applying the shells for a stunning smile. It is always best to consult your prosthodontist who specializes in this cosmetic procedure.

      2. THEY ARE NOT REQUIRED BUT AN OPTION TO IMPROVE YOUR SMILE.

      Smiling is a way for you to communicate. It expresses your emotions and you want it to be perfect as much as possible. Porcelain dental veneers are the perfect option for you if you’re considering for a dental makeover. This is because porcelain has a natural colour similar to your teeth. This also helps correct some minor teeth shape issues to achieve that ideal symmetry.

      3. ONE SMILE DOES NOT FIT ALL.

      These dental veneers are individually shaped to specifically fit each of your bites and ensure a natural contouring result. Your prosthodontist is able to fabricate the shells to cover some gaps or spaces in between your teeth since they are customized.

      4. YOU DON’T NEED VENEERS FOR ALL OF YOUR TEETH.

      This treatment aims to aesthetically improve the appearance of your teeth, however, not all of your teeth need it. The treatment involves trimming down of your teeth, so your healthy teeth need to be spared from the treatment. Only those with deep discolouration and cracking should receive this treatment.

      5. YOU CAN CHOOSE BETWEEN COMPOSITE RESIN OR PORCELAIN FOR YOUR VENEERS.

      Your dentist will provide you with these options when you decide to have the treatment. Porcelain dental veneers are preferred by most prosthodontists since they are more durable and cannot be stained. On the other hand, composite resin or componeers are made from a hard plastic mixture. They are both very durable but componeers are easily stained. Should you prefer the composite resin, you have to choose what you eat to prevent stains on your composite veneer.

      It is important to note that this is not solely for cosmetic purpose. The shells that are bonded to your teeth help them to be protected from further damages. However, the natural teeth behind them are still prone to decay. Regular dental visits are necessary once you have this treatment to keep them in great shape and intact. Moreover, you have to practice great oral care daily.

      The dental veneers cost is dependent on how many teeth would need some coverings. See your dentist today and learn more about his treatment plan to give you a smile makeover.

        Having braces at a later point in your life can be awkward. Many patients avoid wearing them due to being too visible and obvious. Although there is no age requirement when braces can be applied to a patient, it could be embarrassing during adulthood. If you have been needing this treatment but want to avoid the attention, then ceramic braces are for you.

        With the advancement in dentistry, now you can straighten your teeth and avoid being too “in your face” about it. This treatment uses translucent brackets that makes it almost invisible to the eyes. Ceramic braces in Toronto, Ontario provides this option to discreetly align or straighten your teeth.

        DO CERAMIC BRACES FUNCTION LIKE TRADITIONAL BRACES?

        Yes, they have the same function and purpose to help straighten the patient’s teeth, giving him a beautiful smile. The difference between clear ceramic braces and traditional metal braces is that the former’s bracket matches the colour of your natural teeth. Unlike the traditional braces that use metal as brackets, clear braces use ceramic which is translucent enough to be unnoticeable. This is the reason why, more orthodontists recommend ceramic braces to their patients.

        ARE CERAMIC BRACES SUITABLE FOR ALL ORTHODONTIC CASES?

        Your dentist will check your teeth if ceramic aligners is suitable for you. He starts the procedure with an oral prophylaxis to ensure that your teeth are free from plaque. Then, all throughout your treatment, you’ll have series of regular dental check-up in order to monitor and adjust your braces. The duration of the orthodontic treatment will depend on how severe the alignment issues are.

        Clear braces might not be applicable to you if you have serious teeth misalignment. Your dentist will recommend that you have metal braces for this situation. This is because the metal brackets can apply more force to initiate the movement and adjustment of your teeth. Now, let’s talk about the benefits of clear ceramic braces to your teeth.

        THE BENEFITS OF HAVING CERAMIC BRACES

        1. CLEARNESS.

        The shape of the clear braces is similar to the one you see on traditional metal braces. However, the brackets are translucent making it less noticeable to the naked eye. If your dentist puts a clear or tooth-coloured wire to complement the brackets, it will be almost invisible.

        There are possibilities of staining the brackets with food and drinks such as coffee, tea and other coloured substance. So, proper oral care is a must to protect the clearness of your ceramic braces.

        2. DURABILITY.

        Clear braces are made from alumina compounds which are strong and non-porous. It makes the brackets durable and hard to damage like the metal ones. Your orthodontist can make the archwire frosted, silver or white to blend even better on your clear brackets. This also acts as the backbone of the brackets that force your teeth to move to their proper places.

        3. CONVENIENCE

        Since clear braces use smaller brackets than metal braces, they take less space of your front teeth. These smaller brackets are still sturdy and resilient to force. They are easier to remove by your orthodontist after the treatment has been concluded.

        Bonding agents used in metal braces can be hard on your teeth. They sometimes cause some damage to your teeth’s enamel. However, with clear ceramic braces, the bonding compound used does not demineralize your teeth’s enamel. This makes the removal of the brackets easier after the treatment.

        Clear braces are a breakthrough in dentistry which straightens the teeth discreetly. This treatment is just like Invisalign which uses clear mouth trays to align your teeth. The advantage of clear dental braces is that you can get it with almost half the price of an Invisalign.


        To know more about the cost of this treatment, you can always visit your dentist or book an appointment with them. You can also get yours today to get your teeth aligned and to know more about clear braces.

          We will answer this question with one simple answer. A tooth extraction will help you be your best self for your date.

          The new year is kicking off full speed, and we will soon be going outa on dates!

          Being your best self is important. Most of the ladies schedule facial treatments and dress fitting days early for dates. On one hand, most men are working on the restaurant bookings, chocolate shopping, and flower picking.

          You are not your best self when you are suffering from toothaches and even headaches. If you have an impacted wisdom tooth then you need a wisdom tooth extraction. Another cause of extraction is if you have a tooth suffering from decay. Whatever it is, a tooth extraction procedure helps remove the things that are bringing you down.

          When do I need a tooth extraction?

          There are three main reasons why a tooth extraction in Toronto, Ontario happens.

          1. A tooth badly damaged by decay will need extraction when dental restorations can no longer fix it. Sometimes the extent of the damage is too big for a simple filling. In order to protect the other teeth, a tooth extraction happens.
            If this doesn’t convince you, then you have to understand that tooth decay leads to bad breath. Nobody wants a date with bad breath.
          2. An impacted wisdom tooth needs to be extracted because this can cause pain on your other teeth. You’ll not be able to focus on your tasks, or on your date if something is pressing against your other teeth. Other than toothaches, a wisdom tooth extraction also lessens headaches, and other orofacial pain.
          3. Your dentist also does tooth pulling is to make room in the mouth for the other tooth to align properly. This is usually done in connection with orthodontic treatments.

          How long does it take for my gums to heal after a tooth extraction?

          Recovery after a tooth extraction lasts between seven to ten days. However, this will depend on the location and the type of extraction. One amazing thing about the body is that it heals by itself. What you can do during this time is to help speed up the healing time.

          Tooth extraction aftercare

          • It is important to take the prescribed painkillers by your dentist. This helps to relieve any minor discomfort that you may feel after the procedure.
          • Do not remove the initial gauze pad placed. Leave it there for the first four hours after the procedure.
          • Apply an ice bag on the area. You can leave it there for not more than ten minutes at a time. Leaving an ice pack too long can damage the tissues.
          • Rest for the first 24 hours after the extraction. Avoid any strenuous activities for the meantime that might result in bleeding. Examples of this are heavy lifting and even bending.
          • To help your blood clot where the extraction happened, avoid rinsing, spitting or using a drinking straw. This is only for the first 24 hours.
          • For the time being, avoid using a mouthwash as well during the crucial 24 hours.
          • After the first 24 hours are over, rinse your mouth with a solution made of salt and warm water.
          • Consume only soft foods like mashed potatoes and yogurt. Try to chew on the opposite side of your mouth, away from the extraction area.
          • Avoid smoking, if possible, forever. Smoking interrupts the healing process and might cause inflammation. The disadvantages of smoking greatly outweigh the benefits. For the good of your dental health, we encourage you to quit it once and for all.
          • Schedule a follow-up appointment with your dentist to track the progress of your healing.

          If you need a wisdom tooth extraction or any kind of tooth extraction we highly suggest that you do it soon. Schedule an appointment with your dental clinic today.

          You are the best version of yourself when you are happy and you feel good about yourself. When you feel good about yourself, you are more confident in dates.

            Investing in the right solution is important if you have a missing tooth. If you want to invest in something that is sturdy and will last you a long time. Dental implants are a popular solution nowadays because they function and look like regular teeth.

            What are the disadvantages of not replacing a missing tooth?

            • Not replace your missing tooth is a popular cause of jaw bone loss. The roots of a tooth stimulate and support the bone of your jaws. When you lose a tooth, the bone is no longer stimulated, therefore, it can collapse. Titanium dental implants are surgically inserted in the jaw bone to support it and the tooth replacement. This process is also called osseointegration.
            • Missing a tooth can cause difficulty in chewing. This is especially hard if you are missing one tooth in front. Back teeth do all the heavy chewing of meat and other foods, missing them can affect how you eat.
            • As time goes by, teeth shifts because they are trying to fill in the space left by a missing tooth. The shifting of teeth creates more space which might cause other teeth to shift as well. Teeth movement affects your entire bite.
            • A change in your bite can lead to problems like headaches, muscle pain, wearing of the teeth, and even TMJD.
            • Teeth also support the shape of your face. Not replacing a missing tooth can cause your face to appear aged.
            • You can also suffer some difficulty in speaking if you have a missing tooth.

            Why are dental implants a good choice?

            Dental implants in Toronto Ontario has many benefits. The following are what makes them a good choice in replacing a missing tooth.

            • They help improve your appearance. Implants look like your natural teeth. No one will know that they are simply a tooth replacement. They also feel like natural teeth since they have a screw that fuses with the bone to make them permanent.

            • With dental implants, you are also able to speak more properly and clearly. Dentures that do not fit you properly can slip within your mouth. This can cause a mumble or slur in your words. Implants remove the worry that they might slip anytime.
            • Implants do not only improve speaking but also makes eating easier. Dentures that slide out of place can make chewing meat difficult. With implants, you chew food with the comfort of regular teeth.
            • As opposed to removable dentures, titanium dental implants gives you more comfort. The reason for this is because they are like your natural teeth. You do not need to remove and give them special cleaning every night.
            • Oral health greatly improves with implants. It doesn’t need reduction of other teeth like with dental bridges. Your other natural teeth are intact, which improves their longevity. Implants also make cleaning of the teeth easier, improving your oral hygiene.
            • Dental implants are sure to last many years with good oral hygiene and proper care.
            • Improve your self-esteem with implants. You don’t have to worry about your tooth replacement falling apart. It restores normal chewing patterns and when you smile, no one will know the difference.

            Invest in the right treatment when replacing a missing tooth. This is because you will be wearing them for a long time in your life. Titanium dental implants are a great choice. It has many benefits that will make your life easier. Improve your confidence in life by replacing a missing tooth, you will not regret it.

              Crooked and misaligned teeth are common problems under orthodontic dentistry. Traditional and metal braces are the main players in this field. However, because of the advancement in dental technology, a new player has now emerged. Its name is Invisalign.

              If you are someone who needed orthodontic treatment but didn’t want to wear braces then this is for you. It is understandable that some might get conscious with how metal braces look on their teeth. Sometimes they delay the treatment because they are afraid that others might laugh at them because of their braces.

              WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT INVISALIGN

              1. Invisalign clear aligners are custom-made aligners that are almost invisible on your mouth, such that others will hardly notice them. They do the job of correcting your teeth without making you self-conscious with how you look.
              2. You can take out your clear aligners when you brush and floss your teeth. This is a good thing because it ensures that your teeth are clean and healthy. Unlike with traditional braces, it is hard to get through the spaces because of the metal wires. Sometimes eating can be a challenge because some of the food particles can get stuck between. Not getting them out can cause the bacteria on the mouth to feed on the food. This can lead to plaque and decay. With Invisalign, you can take your aligners out when you eat and drink. Recommended time of wearing these aligners is 22 hours a day.
              3. Metal brackets are the common image that pops into mind when someone says traditional braces. These metal wires can sometimes scrape the insides of your mouth. On one hand, the plastic aligners are gentle on both your cheeks and gums.
              4. Having to wear aligners means changing them every two weeks. Wearing this dental appliance follows a treatment plan. This will ensure you to have straighter teeth after a certain period of time. Your dentist will change your Invisalign clear aligners according to the treatment plan. The aligners put pressure on your teeth which means that in a few weeks, your teeth are in a new position. The new aligners will slowly push them to a new position until they reach the right one. Invisalign treatment in Toronto Ontario will not last for more than two years.
              5. If you currently have braces now, you can shift to Invisalign upon the advice of your dentist. Many people have switched to Invisalign because of its almost invisible appearance.
              6. There are only certain dental clinics in Canada that offer the Invisalign treatment. Only dentists who have completed a specialized training can offer this service.
              7. Take care of your aligners with a special cleaning solution that your dentist prescribes. You can also clean them using a toothbrush. Don’t forget to rinse them with lukewarm water. Do not use hot water because it might damage the plastic.
              8. As with many orthodontic treatments, you might have to wear retainers after. This helps prevent your teeth from shifting back to their initial position.

              The Invisalign clear aligners are the game changer in the world of orthodontics. They are the best alternatives to traditional metal braces. They get the job done without damaging your self-confidence.

                    What causes bad breath?

                    • Poor dental hygiene.

                    Improper brushing can cause bad breath. Food particles remain in your mouth if you don’t brush and floss daily. You should also clean your dentures and braces properly, if you’re wearing one. Remove stuck food particles to prevent bad breath.

                    • Having a dry mouth.

                    The dentists at a dental clinic in Toronto attest that dry mouth condition or xerostomia results in halitosis because of low saliva production. Saliva cleanses the mouth and removes particles that cause odor. Having a dry mouth condition may be due to a disease or a salivary gland problem.

                    • Medication.

                    There are some medications that cause dry mouth condition and ultimately bad breath. It include the following: antihistamines, antipsychotics, antidepressants, lung inhalers, Alzheimer’s disease medication and Parkinson’s disease medication.

                    • Tobacco products

                    Smokers and tobacco users are more prone to halitosis and candidates of periodontal disease as well.

                    • Food

                    When food particles are broken down it could emit a foul odor which results in bad breath. Spices like garlic and onions also have strong odors that affect your breath.

                    • Gingivitis

                    Gingivitis, the early stage of gum disease, also attribute to bad breath due to the severely swollen gums. It can be addressed with some of the best natural bad breath remedy options available at home.

                    • Nose, throat and other mouth infections

                    An inflammation in the nose, throat or sinuses may result to postnasal drip and ultimately bad breath. Small stones that form in the tonsils, when filled with bacteria, also cause bad breath. Consult your specialist at the dental clinic in Toronto.

                    • Diseases like cancer and chronic reflux of stomach acids

                    Metabolic disorder, cancer and chronic reflux of stomach acids cause halitosis since these produce certain chemicals. GERD or gastroesophageal reflux disease also results in bad breath.

                    The best natural breath remedy options that works

                    There are various ways to cure halitosis. These are some of the best natural breath remedy options that you can try:

                    • Warm salt water rinse.

                    A saline solution stops bacteria build-up because it has slight acidity and prevents the alkalizing environment which results in microbe growth.

                    • Cinnamon chewing sticks.

                    Cinnamon contains fiber and has an antimicrobial action. It fights bacteria that causes oral infection and it has digestive and respiratory benefits.

                    • Green tea.

                    Green tea contains antioxidants, polyphenols and antibacterial properties. Drinking at least five cups of green tea daily helps fight bad breath.

                    • Aloe vera

                    Aloe vera has natural antifungal and antibacterial contents. You can combat bad breath by boiling water with aloe vera gel and using it as a mouthwash.

                    • Essential oils like peppermint, eucalyptus and lemongrass.

                    These essential oils have antimicrobial ingredients that kill harmful bacteria which cause bad breath. Use these oils as a mouthwash and rinse your mouth regularly with it.

                    • Proper oral hygiene.

                    Get rid of halitosis by following proper oral care like daily brushing and flossing. Most dentists at the dental clinic in Toronto recommend that your tongue should also be cleaned to avoid bacteria build-up.

                    • Manage stress.

                    Chronic stress affects the immune system and creates imbalance. It also increases sweat gland activity which results in bad odor and bad breath.

                    • Get a good night’s sleep and rest.

                    Lack of sleep harms the immune system and disrupts stress response. Just like stress, this could create an imbalance in your body rhythm and cause bad breath.
