Meet Dr. Amelia Chan

Dr. Chan graduated from the University of Western Ontario in 1987. Originally from the London area, Dr. Chan has been based in the downtown Toronto area since 1989. Her areas of interest include Inside Braces (ibraces/Incognito), Porcelain Braces, Alloderm Gum Grafting, Cosmetic Dentistry, Laser Dentistry, and Implants. Dr Chan has recently written a nationally published article “Cancer Care for the Dental Patient”.

GENERAL DENTISTRY

Checkup and Cleaning | Extractions | Fillings | Gum Disease | Halitosis Treatment | Implants | Sedation Dentistry

Our dentist can provide you with the best general dentistry services available. We value your oral health and treat you with the utmost care and compassion.Our routine checkups are designed to identify dental problems so we can give you the care you need to keep your oral health at its best. We clean your teeth regularly to prevent tartar buildup and help you avoid cavities and damage to your teeth and gums.

Lumineers | Teeth Whitening | Veneers
